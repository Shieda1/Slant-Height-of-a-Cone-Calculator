What is the slant height of a cone?
The slant height of a cone is the distance from the vertex to the edge of the circular base of the cone.
How to calculate slant height of a cone?
To calculate slant height of a cone you should use the following formula:
l = (r^2 + h^2)^1/2
where:
r = radius
h = height
Why use slant height of a cone?
The slant height of a cone is used in order to measure the segment connecting the apex of a cone to the outer rim of it's base.
